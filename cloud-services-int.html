Why I Think Cloud Services Are Useful

I like cloud services because they are easy to use and make life easier for everyone as they provide numerous services such as the ability to access your information from anywhere in the world. One of the most beneficial things that cloud services offer is the ability to back up all your data so you do not lose it if for instance your computer breaks.

Some Interesting Facts About Cloud Services

There are four main types of cloud services which are: Software as a Service (SaaS) - Salesforce, Platform as a Service (PaaS) - Cloud 9, Infrastructure as a Service (IaaS) - Amazon and also Communication as a Service (CaaS) - Gmail.

Cloud Services are used by millions of people daily to store data, purchase goods and also as a means of communication.

Without cloud services, our information/data would be more at risk of being lost and/or deleted.
